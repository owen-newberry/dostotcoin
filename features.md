Example
You have 513 DostotCoin in your wallet.

### dst coinflip (#loot-lake)
- Is gambling a wager on a coinflip, calling heads or tails
- If they win, they get double their wager
- If they lose, they lose their wager
- Requires this format
- dst coinflip (wager as an int) (h or t for heads or tails)

Example
dst coinflip 2 t

It was Tails! You won 2 D$T!
You have 25 DostotCoin in your wallet.
